Anxiety Management

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a feeling of worry, nervousness, or unease about something with an uncertain outcome. It can manifest in various ways, affecting both mental and physical health.

Common Symptoms of Anxiety

  • Restlessness or feeling on edge
  • Fatigue
  • Difficulties concentrating
  • Irritability
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Physical symptoms such as increased heart rate or sweating

Effective Anxiety Management Techniques

  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Regular physical activity
  • Mindfulness and meditation
  • Maintaining a healthy diet
  • Seeking support from friends and family
  • Professional help, such as therapy or counseling
